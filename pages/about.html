About

These are my personal flash card decks I've used to increase my recall of various front end topics. The idea came to me when I was reading about the Sans Forgetica project. The font makes a perfect pairing with flash cards.

Sans Forgetica uses 'desired-difficulty' to challenge the brain. This is theorized to increase the cognitive effort thus improving the retention and recall of information intake. Overall, this should make your memory training with flash cards a more challenging experience to make the terms stick around longer. It also might just give you a head ache from reading a broken font.

Optician Sans is based on optician charts used for vision testing. I use it for chaper titles in the decks and it makes a great visual contrast for readability.

Flash cards are a great way to increase retention and recall using spaced repetition. These decks aren't just all terms and definitions. They contain best practices, useful tips and summaries of technical knowledge that assist with being a better developer/designer. But, they don't replace the actual doing and learning process of building and fixing websites. I hope you them useful.
